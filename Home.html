As we all know Egpyt is famous for the Pryramids and millions of people all across the world come to visit and see the Pharoh's body.

According to a report released by the Central Bank of Egypt (CBE), Egypt's tourist revenue increased by 43.5 % to a record-breaking 4.1 billion US dollars in the first quarter of the financial year 2022-2023,
which runs from July to September of last year.

According to the statement, the number of visitors travelling to Egypt from July through September of last year jumped by 52.2 percent to 3.4 million, and the number of nights they stayed increased by 47.1 percent to 43.6 million.
